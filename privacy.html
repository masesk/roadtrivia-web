Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Introduction

Road Trivia ("we", "our", or "us") is a trivia game application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the Road Trivia mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to being transparent about our practices.

Information We Collect

We do not directly collect or store personal information on our servers. The App offers login functionality through Facebook, which is handled entirely by the Facebook SDK. Any information collected through Facebook login (such as your name or profile picture) is governed by Facebook's Privacy Policy. Game data such as scores and progress is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Facebook SDK

The App integrates the Facebook SDK to provide login functionality. By logging in with Facebook, you authorize Facebook to share certain profile information with the App as permitted by your Facebook privacy settings. We do not control Facebook's data collection practices. For more information, please review Facebook's Privacy Policy and your Facebook privacy settings.

Advertising

The App displays advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These ad networks may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar technologies to serve ads that are relevant to your interests, based on your activity within the App and elsewhere. We do not sell your personal information to advertisers. The data practices of our ad network partners are governed by their own privacy policies.

Data Storage

Any game-related data (such as your scores and preferences) is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this data to external servers.

Data Sharing and Selling

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Information may be shared only as described in this policy (e.g., via the Facebook SDK and advertising partners) or if required by law.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@roadtrivia.com.
